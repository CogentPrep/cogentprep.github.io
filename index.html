What we do

Having shared the stressful experience of taking standardized tests, we strive to make this journey smoother for others. CogentPrep aims to provide college preparatory resources to high school athletes and low-income students. We are an organization that focuses on helping students reach their goals with PSAT/SAT readiness and general tutoring.
