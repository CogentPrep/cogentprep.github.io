What we do

Tutoring for students, by students.

CogentPrep aims to connect high school students with other high school student tutors. Our program is focused on creating a network of student resources to high school athletes and low-income students who struggle to reach the SAT score they need for that scholarship. We are a nonprofit organization that focuses primarily on PSAT and SAT readiness for high schoolers unable to invest money into expensive preparatory programs.
